About Us

Who are we?

We are an Mexico based food delivery company. We are a team of professionals who are passionate about food and delivering the best quality food to our customers.

We at Los Pollos Hermanos have been in the food delivery business for over 10 years. Our founder and CEO, Gustavo Fring, started the company in 2008 with a single restaurant in Albuquerque, New Mexico. Since then, we have grown to over 100 restaurants in 10 cities across the country.
